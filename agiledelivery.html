Agile Delivery

Agile delivery is a critical capability in modern digital organisations such as MindSpring Collective. It enables teams to deliver value iteratively, respond quickly to changing user needs, and continuously improve products and services. Through structured collaboration, regular feedback, and incremental delivery, Agile approaches ensure that solutions are both effective and user-centred.

I have applied Agile delivery principles across a range of digital initiatives, ensuring that projects are delivered efficiently while maintaining alignment with organisational objectives and user needs.

Project Examples

Intranet Implementation

I led the delivery of a new organisational intranet designed to improve internal communication, knowledge sharing, and collaboration across teams.

The project followed Agile principles, with iterative releases and regular stakeholder feedback to ensure the solution evolved based on user needs.

Mobile App for Longitudinal Recovery Support

I contributed to the delivery of a mobile application to support longitudinal recovery for MindSpring Collective’s clients.

Using Agile methods, the team delivered features incrementally, allowing continuous testing, feedback, and improvement of the user experience.
