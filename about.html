MindSpring Collective

Australia's only digital first mental health service

MindSpring Collective is a digital‑first mental health service built for the modern world - where care, connection, and clarity meet online. Founded by clinicians and designers who believe technology can deepen empathy rather than replace it, MindSpring reimagines support as accessible, intuitive, and human. The name evokes renewal and flow: a spring of fresh ideas and emotional energy. Through evidence‑based programs, interactive tools, and creative storytelling, the collective helps people rediscover momentum and meaning. It’s not just therapy - it’s a digital ecosystem for growth, designed to make mental wellness feel fluid, personal, and part of everyday life.

  • Innovation is at our heart, driving us to design digital experiences that make mental health support more intuitive, accessible, and human.
  • Empathy guides every interaction, ensuring our technology deepens understanding rather than distancing people from care.
  • Evidence shapes our decisions, grounding every tool, program, and feature in research‑backed methods that genuinely help people grow.
  • Accessibility is our promise, removing barriers so anyone can find support—anytime, anywhere, on their terms.
  • Collective growth defines us, because we believe mental wellbeing strengthens when people, communities, and ideas evolve together.
